
LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is made and entered into as of this day between the Landlord and the Tenant. 
Tenant agrees to rent the premises for a term of 12 months beginning on June 1, 2025. 
Tenant agrees to pay monthly rent in the amount of $1,200. 
Tenant is responsible for electricity and internet utilities.

EARLY TERMINATION:
Tenant may terminate the lease early with a 60-day written notice and a fee equal to one month’s rent.

MAINTENANCE:
The Landlord is responsible for structural maintenance. Tenant must maintain cleanliness and report issues.

AUTOMATIC RENEWAL:
This lease will automatically renew unless either party gives 30 days’ notice before the end of the lease term.
